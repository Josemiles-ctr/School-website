Welcome to Our Academics Page

At Mengo Senior School, we are dedicated to providing quality education that equips our students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to excel in both local and global communities. Our academic programs are designed to follow the guidelines set by the Uganda National Examination Board (UNEB) while embracing innovative teaching practices that prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum is rooted in Uganda's national education framework, ensuring that students are not only well-prepared for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE), and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE), but also empowered to think critically and creatively.

Secondary School (S1-S4)

In Secondary, we offer a broad range of subjects including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Geography, and History. We also offer specialized subjects such as Computer Studies, Agriculture, and Home Economics, allowing students to explore their areas of interest.

A-Level (S5-S6)

Our A-Level programs offer various subject combinations based on student interests and strengths. Students can choose from:

  • Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics
  • Humanities: Literature, History, Geography, Economics
  • Commerce: Accounting, Business Studies, Economics

Special Programs

At Mengo Senior School, we believe in offering more than just traditional classroom learning. We have a range of specialized programs to support our students in exploring their talents and preparing for the future:

  • Vocational Training: Our technical programs in areas such as Carpentry, Electrical Engineering, and Tailoring provide practical skills that are highly valued in today’s job market.
  • STEM Initiatives: We are passionate about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Our students have access to exciting hands-on projects, coding workshops, and science fairs that encourage curiosity and innovation.
  • Arts & Sports: Students can also explore their creative side through music, drama, and fine arts programs. Our sports teams offer opportunities for students to develop physical skills, teamwork, and discipline.

Academic Support

We understand that each student has a unique learning style, and we provide support to help every learner succeed:

  • Remedial Classes & Tutoring: For students who may need extra help in certain subjects, we offer remedial classes and after-school tutoring to ensure no one gets left behind.
  • Guidance and Counseling: Our counseling services help students manage academic stress, plan for their future careers, and navigate personal challenges.
  • Special Education Services: We are committed to an inclusive education system and provide tailored support for students with disabilities or learning difficulties.

Assessment And Grading

We follow the Uganda National Examination Board's (UNEB) grading system for both O and A level. Our students are assessed through a combination of continuous assessments, term exams, and national examinations:

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE): For Secondary 4 students (O-Level).
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE): For Secondary 6 students (A-Level).

    • We also conduct regular internal assessments to track students' progress throughout the academic year.

Extracurricular And Enrichment Programs

At Mengo Senior School, we recognize that education extends beyond the classroom. We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to enrich the student experience:

  • Clubs and Societies: We have a variety of clubs such as the Debate Club, Science Club, and Drama Society, where students can explore their interests and develop their talents.
  • Sports: Our students are encouraged to participate in sports like football, athletics, and netball, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.
  • Academic Competitions: We regularly organize inter-school academic competitions, including science fairs, quizzes, and debates, where students can showcase their knowledge and compete at the regional or national level.

Accreditation & Recognition

Our school is accredited by the Uganda National Examination Board (UNEB), ensuring that our students receive a recognized and standardized education. We are proud of the many academic accolades our students have earned over the years, from top performers in national exams to winners of regional academic competitions.

Preparing For The Future

We prepare students for the world beyond the classroom. We provide career counseling, university application guidance, and internships that help students explore different career paths. Our graduates have gone on to attend some of Uganda’s top universities and institutions, pursuing studies in fields ranging from medicine and engineering to business and the arts.
