ABOUT US PAGE

Mengo Senior Secondary is a renowned educational institution located in kampala, Uganda. With a rich history spanning over 120 years, we have established ourselves as a center of academic excellence, producing well-rounded individuals who excel in various fields.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that empowers students to become critical thinkers, innovative problem solvers, and responsible citizens. We strive to create a supportive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and character development.

Our Values

At Mengo SSS, we uphold the following values:

Academic Excellence

We pursue academic excellence through rigorous teaching, learning, and assessment.

Integrity

We promote honesty, transparency and accountability in all endavours.

Respect

We foster a culture of respect for diversity, inclusivity and individuality.

Responsibility

We encourage students to take ownership of their learning, actions and decisions.

Community

We build strong relationships with our stakeholders, including students, parents, teachers and wider community.

Our History

Mengo SSS was founded in 1895 by the Church Missionary Society (CMS). Over the years, the school has undergone significant transformations, expanding it's curriculum, infrastructure and student body. Today, we are thriving intitution with a strong reputation for academic excellence and character development.
